Equal Road Book | Signage

A guide book to a hypothetical timeline installed throughout Boston, MA meant to create a more aware commuter. The subject matter would be memorializing cyclist fatalities as well as celebrating progress for cycling in Boston. The content and page inserts within the book reference the actual dimensions of the proposed signage.
