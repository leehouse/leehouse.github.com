Hi! I am a student at MassArt and a founder of the MassArt Outdoor Adventure Club. Focusing on equal parts visual beauty and conceptual strength, I find that my work manifests itself through design using word and image. Each day, I take new steps to seek and discover the point where my passions for art and the outdoors intersect. This site is dedicated to the growth and exploration of these interests. Drop me a line if you want to collaborate on a project or go for a hike.
